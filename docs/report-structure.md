# CloudReady Assessment — Report Structure

## Overview

The full report is generated by Azure OpenAI based on assessment answers and calculated scores.
It contains 10 sections designed to serve as a decision-grade consulting output, not just a data summary.

---

## Sections

| # | Section | Purpose | Source |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Company Overview | Industry + Size + IT Maturity (Low/Medium/High) | AI generated |
| 2 | Cloud Readiness Score | Score + Level + Breakdown per category + Contextual insights | Calculated + AI |
| 3 | Cloud Maturity Position | One sentence summarizing the company's current position | AI generated |
| 4 | Key Findings | Facts detected — mix of risks and strengths | AI generated |
| 5 | Risk Assessment | Business impact per risk + mitigation steps (High/Medium/Low) | AI generated |
| 6 | Recommended Azure Services | Service + outcome framing + why it fits + use case | AI generated |
| 7 | Architecture Suggestion | Component → Azure Service mapping, grouped by layer | AI generated |
| 8 | Migration Roadmap | 3 phases + timeline + total estimated duration | AI generated |
| 9 | Estimated Cost Range | Min/max range + cost of NOT migrating + ROI estimate | AI generated |
| 10 | Next Steps | 3–5 actionable guided steps with KlayyTech | AI generated |

---

## Key Design Decisions

### Findings vs Risks — different roles
- **Findings** = facts detected from answers (no interpretation)
- **Risks** = business impact of those facts (consequences, not repetition)

### Score Section — decision driver, not just a number
Each score includes:
- Comparison to industry average
- Biggest gap identified
- Actionable insight (e.g., "fixing X alone could raise score to Y")

### Services Section — outcome framing
Each service leads with a business outcome before the technical reason:
- ✅ "Prevents data loss and ensures recovery within minutes"
- ❌ "Automated backup service"

### Architecture — layered, not linear
Grouped into App Layer / Data Layer / Security Layer for clarity.

### Cost Section — ROI framing
Shows both:
- Monthly cloud cost range
- Cost of NOT migrating (downtime risk + overhead reduction)

### CTA Design — conversion layer
Hero block at the top with dynamic CTAs based on score level:
- Score < 60: "Start your pilot migration plan" + "Fix critical security gaps"
- Score 60–75: "Request detailed architecture" + "Talk to a cloud expert"
- Score > 75: "Start full migration" + "Get a detailed quote"
